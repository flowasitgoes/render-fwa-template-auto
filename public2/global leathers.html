WhyGlobalLeathers?

Let's get started

  • 40+ Years of Experience

    Our knowledge and expertise, built upon since 1980, allows us to simplify complex processes and guide our clients effectively. Our system is designed to be quick and simple.

  • More Than Just Business

    We pride ourselves on helping the overall leather community grow and thrive. We treat our customers like family and want to continue building strong, trusting relationships.

  • Finest Quality and Widest Selection

    We offer unique and high-quality articles. Our leather is ethically sourced, consistent, durable, and versatile across all industries. We take pride in our endless leather sourcing; focusing primarily on the latest trends and LWG compliance requirements. Tannery sustainability practices are vital to us, and we explore their carbon footprint before purchasing.

  • Service, Service, Service

    We provide expert consultations and guide our clients in the right direction. It’s important to make our designers feel comfortable, heard, seen, and motivated. We’re able to accommodate individual customers’ wants and needs. We’re always more than willing to provide samples and answer any project-related questions.

Introduction

About Global Leathers

  • Company Overview

    Founded in 1980, Global Leathers is a second-generation family-owned business that supplies premium upholstery leathers to the interior design and architect community. We are the epitome of high-quality, service-oriented leather sourcing.

    Our leathers are sourced from the best leather. Working Group compliant tanneries, ensuring sustainable practices and adherence to LWG environmental standards.

  • Expertise and Versatility

    Beyond upholstery leather, we have vast experience in garment, handbag, and accessory leathers.

    Our leathers have been featured in Broadway Performances, Fashion Brand Collections, Music Videos, TV Shows + Movies, Cruise Ships, and Celebrity Residences.

  • Commitment to Excellence

    The Crystal family’s passion for sourcing the finest leathers and staying updated with the latest design trends drives Global Leathers’ pursuit of excellence.

    Over the years, our leather has been featured in renowned publications, such as Architectural Digest, Interior Design Market Tabloid, Homes & Gardens, The New York Times, and Nylon Magazine showcasing our leather quality and sourcing expertise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a minimum order?

MOQ is one (1) hide

Are custom orders possible?

Custom orders are always possible

What is the lead time for a CFA?

Cuttings for approval typically take 1 day – 2 weeks depending on the article/color. CFAs are highly recommended for all leathers due to dye lot variations

What is the lead time for shipping in-stock leather?

In stock leather typically ships between 1 day – 2 weeks depending on the article/color

What is your return policy?

You have 30 days to dispute and/or return hides. We will not accept hides that have been cut or treated

What are your account terms?

We must receive advanced full invoice payment prior to shipment

Do you offer payment terms?

Terms are considered on a case-by-case basis

What is your FOB point?

New York, NY | Secaucus, NJ
