Brand

The brand focuses on the cube's shape and build, the contrast between the desktop work and the scenery outdoors. It's all about making a unique third place to treat your inner hermit.

Website

Quidrix is all about clean shapes, connection with nature, and customization. "The website must evoke curiosity and attention to detail," we thought. Hence, big titles, lots of space, and a wizard to imagine exactly the space you need. And as a cherry on top, you get your 3D model augment a beautiful outback location in just three months.

Wizard

And now, the fun part. The wizard made the Qudrix concept a reality! Instead of just selling an outdoor cube idea, Qudrix helped customers sculpt their own space and define their expectations. The brand's promise is that you'll see this project on your land in just 3 months (plus shipping).

3D Modeling

To envision the cube in serene natural surroundings, 3D models are essential. All of the exterior 3D renders are made by hi atelier studio.
