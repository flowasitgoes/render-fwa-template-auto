Phantom is a technology-led creative agency crafting experiences for global brands.

Phantom is an award-winning digital studio that partners with global brands to create impactful experiences at the intersection of technology and creativity. We specialise in crafting innovative digital solutions, from immersive websites and interactive installations to strategic branding and viral campaigns. Leveraging technologies like AI, WebGL, and augmented reality, Phantom delivers elegant, user-centred design and end-to-end development. With studios in London and Auckland, we combine international perspectives with local expertise. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures measurable results, transforming complex challenges into engaging digital experiences that build lasting brand connections.
