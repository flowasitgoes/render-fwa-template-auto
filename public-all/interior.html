Silk and Stone

Silk and Stone

01

What services do you offer at ISRA Design?

ISRA Design offers a full range of interior design services, tailored for both the residential and commercial sectors, with a focus on high-end projects. Services include conceptual design, expert consultancy, project management and complete execution from first sketch to final delivery. We also handle the coordination of every detail, including material selection, custom furnishings and the integration of innovative technological solutions to create unique and functional spaces.

In addition to interior design services, we also offer custom furniture design, space optimization consultancy and interior styling services, ensuring that each project perfectly reflects the client’s personality and requirements.

02

Do I need to be involved in every detail of the design process?

Involvement in the design process depends on the chosen service package. For those who prefer to save time and avoid stress, we offer turn-key project services and customized procurement concierge, ensuring an effortless experience. In these cases, the client’s role is more one of approving and overseeing key milestones, with the client always in control of the decision-making.

In other words, we become your ‘advocate’ throughout the entire interior design process, managing every detail and protecting your interests so that you can enjoy a perfect end result without getting involved in every logistical detail.

03

What interior design styles do you approach?

ISRA Design addresses a wide range of interior design styles, from Wabi Sabi and Japandi to Glamour, Industrial, Luxury, Minimalism, Nordic and Contemporary. Each project is customized to reflect the client’s preferences and the unique characteristics of the space.

We are recognized for creating our own style – Industrial Luxury. It combines cool materials such as metal, concrete and stone with warm elements such as marble, leather and wood, resulting in a unique aesthetic balance. This style defines timeless spaces with strong visual impact and subtle sophistication.

04

I want design for bedroom and living area, could it be done?

To ensure that each project reflects our vision and remains true to ISRA’s signature style, we do not accept partial projects, limited to specific areas of the home. We believe that a successful design requires consistency and aesthetic continuity throughout all spaces, and an integrated design allows us to deliver a harmonious and unified result.

Imagine a perfectly appointed living room and kitchen, but the rest of the house without the same standard – things would be out of sync. That’s why we only collaborate on complete projects, where we can transform every detail, ensuring a flawless design from start to finish.

05

What is the optimal budget we should have?

The optimal budget for a complete home renovation varies depending on the surface area, finishes and complexity of the project. Each project is unique, but to give a clear picture of the investment required, we provide clients with case studies based on similar residential and commercial projects we have completed. This way, clients can understand exactly what services and results they get for the money they invest.

In general, for a complete renovation project, we recommend a budget that reflects the level of quality desired (medium, premium or luxury) and the type of finishes selected. This transparency helps set realistic expectations and ensure a stress-free design experience.
